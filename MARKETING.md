### What It Does

ListingJet is an AI pipeline that turns raw listing photos into a complete marketing package: MLS-ready descriptions, hero photo selection, flyers, social posts, videos, and ads. Upload photos -> AI does the rest.

### How It's Different

Other tools offer individual services (AI photo editing, AI copywriting, flyer generators). ListingJet uses **AI vision to analyze every photo, automatically pick the best hero and supporting shots, then generate the entire marketing package** -- all from the same source material. One upload, complete package.

### Core Capabilities

- AI vision analyzes room types, quality, lighting, composition
- Auto-curates the best photos per channel (MLS, social, ads)
- Generates MLS-safe + marketing copy using Claude
- Creates branded flyers via Canva integration
- Produces listing videos with AI voiceover
- Generates social cuts for Instagram/Facebook/TikTok
- Per-tenant brand kits (colors, logos, agent headshots)

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Primary: Active Real Estate Agents**
- Solo or small-team agents doing 2-20+ listings/month
- US-based (starting US-only; global expansion later)
- Currently cobbling together: photographer + Canva + Dropbox + manual copy + a VA for social posts
- Time-poor, listing-heavy, marketing-budget-constrained
- Pain: "I spend more time on marketing materials than actually selling"

**Secondary: Real Estate Photographers**

**Tertiary: Brokerage Marketing Leads**
- Brokerages with 10+ agents wanting standardized marketing output
- Team admin managing brand consistency across agents
- Pain: "Every agent's listing marketing looks different and off-brand"

**Not our ICP (yet):** Commercial RE brokers, international agents, FSBO, new agents with <1 listing/month.

## Competitor Intelligence

**Source:** `sam-product-marketer/outputs/competitor-report-2026-04-05.md`

### Executive Summary

ListingJet enters a fragmented market where no single competitor offers the complete listing marketing package. Each competitor owns one slice -- descriptions, photo editing, video, or staging. The DIY alternative (Canva + Dropbox + VA) is expensive ($500-1500/mo) and slow. ListingJet's "one upload, complete package" positioning has no direct competitor.

**Overall threat level:** LOW-MEDIUM. The market is fragmented, not consolidated. The risk is not a single dominant player -- it's agents who don't yet know this category exists.

### Competitor Analysis

**1. ListingAI**
- What: AI-generated listing descriptions (text only)
- Pricing: ~$14-34/mo
- Threat: LOW -- single-purpose (descriptions only)
- Key: Don't compete on descriptions alone. Always show the full package.

**2. BoxBrownie**
- What: Photo editing, virtual staging, floor plans (human-edited)
- Pricing: Per-image ($1.60 - $176/image), no subscription
- Threat: MEDIUM -- strong brand, but no copy/flyers/video/social
- Key: Different use case. If agents use BoxBrownie for staging, they still need ListingJet.

**3. Autoreel**
- What: AI-generated listing videos
- Pricing: $19-449/mo
- Threat: LOW-MEDIUM -- video-only
- Key: Video is a strong hook. Lead with "video included" in comparisons.

**4. PhotoAIVideo.com**
- What: Photo-to-video conversion
- Pricing: ~$7.99/video or ~$37.50/mo
- Threat: LOW -- narrow scope

**5. DIY Stack (Canva + Dropbox + VA)**
- What: Manual assembly using multiple tools and human labor
- Cost: $27/mo (tools only) to $1,527/mo (tools + VA)
- Threat: MEDIUM -- this is the status quo and primary competitive target
- Key: Every message should contrast "90 seconds vs. 2 hours" and "$29/mo vs. $500-1500/mo"

### Competitive Pricing Position

| Competitor | Monthly Cost | What You Get |
|------------|-------------|--------------|
| ListingAI | $14-34/mo | Descriptions only |
| BoxBrownie | $50-500+/mo (volume dependent) | Photo editing only |
| Autoreel | $19-449/mo | Video only |
| PhotoAIVideo | $7.99/video or ~$37.50/mo | Video only |
| DIY Stack | $27-1,527/mo | Everything, manually |
| **ListingJet** | **$29/mo** | **Everything, automatically** |

**Piecemeal equivalent:** To replicate ListingJet's output using competitors, an agent would spend $193-483/mo. ListingJet is 6-17x cheaper.

### Key Takeaways

1. **No direct competitor** exists for the "one upload, complete package" positioning.
2. **Biggest threat is inertia**, not a competitor.
3. **BoxBrownie has the strongest brand** in real estate photo services, but doesn't overlap with our core offering.
4. **Video is the hook** -- hardest thing for agents to DIY, included at $29/mo vs. $19-449/mo standalone.
5. **Canva is the long-term watch item** -- if they build real estate-specific workflows, reassess.

### Recommendations

- Launch messaging should target the DIY stack, not individual competitors.
- Lead with video in demos -- highest-friction item for agents to DIY.
- Don't engage in feature-by-feature comparisons with single-purpose tools.
- Monitor Canva's AI roadmap quarterly.
- Build the "listing marketing automation" category in SEO and content before anyone else claims it.

### Complete Funnel Definition

```
VISIT -> SIGNUP -> ACTIVATE -> CONVERT -> EXPAND -> REFER
```

| Stage | Definition | Target Rate |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Visit -> Signup | Creates account | 3-5% (baseline), 5-8% (optimized) |
| Signup -> Activate | Completes first listing package | 40-50% (baseline), 50-60% (optimized) |
| Activate -> Convert | Subscribes to paid plan | 15-20% (baseline), 20-30% (optimized) |
| Convert -> Expand | Upgrades plan or adds add-on | 10-15% within 90 days |
| Any -> Refer | Invites another agent | 5-10% |

### Diagnostic Thresholds

| Stage | Green | Yellow | Red |
|-------|-------|--------|-----|
| Visit -> Signup | > 5% | 3-5% | < 3% |
| Signup -> Activate | > 45% | 30-45% | < 30% |
| Activate -> Convert | > 35% | 20-35% | < 20% |
| Monthly Churn | < 5% | 5-8% | > 8% |

### Homepage CRO Priorities

- Single "Try free" CTA above the fold (not "Learn more")
- Headline passes 5-second test
- Before/after demo GIF showing photos-in, marketing-package-out
- Social proof block visible without scrolling
- Mobile CTA is sticky or repeated after each scroll section
- Page load < 2.5s (LCP)
- Exit-intent popup for non-converters

### Signup Flow Recommendations

- Collect ONLY email + password (or Google/Apple OAuth)
- Google OAuth should be visually dominant
- Let users in immediately; email verification in background
- Never more than 3 steps before first output
- Mobile-first: large tap targets, autofill-friendly

### Onboarding: First Upload to Aha Moment

1. **Welcome + Single Action** (0-10s after signup): "Let's create your first listing package." + "Upload your listing photos" CTA. Offer sample photos if none handy.
2. **Photo Upload** (10-60s): Drag-and-drop, bulk upload, encouraging microcopy.
3. **AI Processing** (60-120s): Step-by-step progress (NOT blank spinner). "Selecting best exterior shots... Writing MLS description..."
4. **Results Reveal** (2-3 min after signup): Full marketing package. This should feel like unwrapping a gift.
5. **Profile Completion** (post-aha): NOW ask for name, brokerage, branding. Converts at 3-5x vs. asking before aha.

- Display: Free Demo -> Lite ($9) -> Active Agent ($29, highlighted "Most Popular") -> Team ($99)

### Customer Health Score Model

```
Health Score (0-100) =
  Login frequency / expected     * 30 +
  Listings this period / last    * 30 +
  Features used / total          * 20 +
  Days since last ticket (cap 30) / 30 * 10 +
  Satisfaction signal             * 10
```

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 80-100 | Green (Healthy) | Nurture: tips, testimonial/referral request |
| 50-79 | Yellow (At Risk) | Engage: re-engagement email, offer help |
| 0-49 | Red (Critical) | Save: personal outreach, save offer, feedback request |
